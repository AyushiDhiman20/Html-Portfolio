My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Shershah

This is my Favourite Patriotic Movie. One of the best Movie I had ever seen.

URI: The Surgical Strike

A fictionally dramatised account of the true event of the retaliation to the 2016 Uri attack.

Sardar Udham

The film follows a non-linear narrative, going back and forth from the present, where Sardar Udham is in London, to his past when he was part of the Hindustan Socialist Republican Association(HSRA) and explores his reasons for assassinating Indian Civil Service officer Micheal O'Dwyer.
